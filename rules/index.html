DutchRP Server Rules

By playing on DutchRP you automatically agree to these rules.

1. General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff at all times.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech is not tolerated.
  • Staff decisions are final. You may appeal calmly via Discord.
  • Do not exploit bugs or game mechanics.

2. Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in character at all times unless instructed otherwise.
  • No Random Deathmatch (RDM).
  • No Random Arrest (RA).
  • FearRP must be followed at all times.
  • Value your life realistically.

3. New Life Rule (NLR)

  • After death, you may not return to the same situation.
  • You forget all events leading up to your death.
  • NLR lasts until the situation is fully over.

4. Jobs & Gameplay

  • Follow your job description at all times.
  • Do not abuse job powers or equipment.
  • Changing jobs to gain advantage is not allowed.

5. Metagaming & Powergaming

  • No metagaming (using out-of-character information).
  • No powergaming (unrealistic actions).
  • Voice, text, and external tools count as metagaming.

6. Communication

  • Use voice chat realistically.
  • No mic spam, soundboards, or voice changers.
  • English or Dutch only.
