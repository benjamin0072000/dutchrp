DutchRP Server Rules

By playing on DutchRP you automatically agree to these rules.

1. General Rules

  • Be respectful to all players and staff. No harassment, racism, sexism, excessive toxicity or hate speech.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times. If you disagree, use reports in Discord — not in public chat.
  • No exploiting bugs, glitches or loopholes. Report them to staff via Discord ticket instead.
  • No cheating, hacking, scripting or use of unfair advantages.
  • Use common sense.

2. Roleplay Rules

  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch). You must have a valid roleplay reason to kill someone.
  • No NLR (New Life Rule). After dying, you may not return to the situation for 5 minutes.
  • No FailRP. Your actions must make sense within the roleplay.
  • No Metagaming. Do not use OOC information IC (Discord, chat, streams, etc.).
  • No Powergaming. Don’t force unrealistic actions or outcomes on others.
  • FearRP applies. You must value your life when threatened.
  • Use /advert before Raiding, Mugging, Kidnapping or Warning players.
  • While mugging the maximum amount is 5K with a 15 min cooldown.
  • Before killing a player you must warn them 3 times in /advert (when loitering, being annoying) Receiving player must FearRP.

3. Combat & Raiding

  • KOS (Kill on Sight) is not allowed unless clearly stated by textsign with a clear border.
  • Do not raid the same player or base within 30min.
  • During a raid, do not disconnect to avoid consequences.
  • Shooting windows (fading doors) must stay open for 4 seconds to allow fair combat.

4. Jobs & Abuse

  • Play your job as intended.
  • No job abuse (e.g. switching jobs to gain an advantage).
  • Police and government jobs must follow server laws and procedures.
  • Corruption is not allowed UNLESS you get bribed by Mocro Mafia members (You may chose the amount).
  • Be sure to read the job description providing useful tips and extra job rules.

5. Props, Tools & Building

  • No prop blocking, prop killing or prop pushing.
  • No prop climbing to gain unfair access.
  • No blocking public areas, streets or important buildings.
  • Don't build on the street if you are not a homeless person.
  • Builds must be reasonable and not excessively hard to raid.
  • You may only have max 3 fading doors in your base. exl. shooting windows.
  • Shooting windows need to be at least 3x3 in size using standaard prop sizes. Also see rule 15 about window combat.
  • No abusing tools, exploits or physics.

6. Communication

  • English and Dutch only.
  • No mic or chat spam unless allowed by job.
  • No advertising other servers or services.

7. Job specific

  • Hooligan rival groups can freely RDM each other.
  • Street artist may build a stage in public.
  • Terrorist are KOS, and when dead not allowed to rejoin job for 1 hour. He must also advert terror before killing.

8. Final Notes

  • These rules apply to everyone, including staff.
  • Rules may change — staying updated is your responsibility.
