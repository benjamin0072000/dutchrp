DutchRP Rules

By playing on DutchRP you automatically agree to these rules.

1. Community & Conduct

  • Respect all players and staff. Harassment, racism, sexism, excessive toxicity or hate speech is not tolerated.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times. If you disagree, open a Discord report — do not argue in public chat.
  • No exploiting bugs, glitches or loopholes. Report them via a Discord ticket.
  • No cheating, hacking, scripting or unfair advantages.
  • Use common sense at all times.

2. Roleplay Standards

  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch). You must have a valid roleplay reason to kill someone.
  • No NLR (New Life Rule). After dying, you may not return to the same situation for 5 minutes.
  • No FailRP. Your actions must make sense within the roleplay environment.
  • No Metagaming. Do not use OOC information (Discord, streams, etc.) in-character.
  • No Powergaming. Do not force unrealistic actions or outcomes on others.
  • FearRP applies at all times. You must value your life when threatened.
  • Use /advert before raiding, mugging, kidnapping or issuing warnings.
  • Before killing for loitering/annoying behavior, you must give 3 clear advert warnings.

3. Criminal Actions & Limits

  • Kidnapping ransom limits: Civilians max €10,000 — Mayor max €25,000.
  • Mugging limit is €5,000 with a 15-minute cooldown.
  • Countering events (raid, mug, kidnap) is not allowed.
  • Do not disconnect during a raid to avoid consequences.

4. Raiding & Combat Rules

  • KOS is only allowed if clearly marked with a visible bordered sign.
  • You may not raid the same base within 30 minutes.
  • Do not assist raids unless you are clearly affiliated (matching name/tag).
  • You must end raids with "/advert Raid Ended" (include gang name if applicable).
  • No building during raids. PD must prepare before the raid starts.
  • Do not raid players who are actively building with a valid building sign.
  • Police do not advert raids — they must obtain a valid warrant.
  • Do not use raids as an excuse to kill. Avoid unnecessary kills when possible.
  • Police require visual proof for warrants. Sparks through walls or assumptions are invalid.
  • Police may not receive assistance from non-government jobs during raids.
  • You may only raid PD with a valid RP reason.
  • Do not interfere with raids unless involved. PD may arrest you, and raiders may eliminate witnesses.

5. Building & Base Rules

  • No prop blocking, prop killing, prop pushing or prop climbing.
  • No blocking public areas or streets (unless PD checkpoint approved by an Admin).
  • Your base must have at least one clear, accessible entrance.
  • No trap bases. Players must be able to exit the same way they entered.
  • No crouch entrances, tunnels, mazes, kill boxes or color rooms.
  • No hidden or non-obvious no-collided walls.
  • No no-collided entrances. Main entrance must be a visible door or fading door.
  • No roof bases. Your base must be inside a building.
  • No unfair defenses where raiders cannot shoot back.
  • Maximum 3 fading doors per base (excluding shooting windows).
  • Fading door hold time must be 5 seconds.
  • Maximum 1 fake keypad per fading door. Keypads must be visible and close to the door.
  • Shooting windows must be at least 3x3 standard prop size and remain open 4 seconds during combat.
  • The fading door tool is for doors only — not bridges or exploit builds.
  • Building signs are allowed while building, but no printers or illegal items may be inside.

6. Jobs & Role Abuse

  • Play your job as intended.
  • No job abuse (switching jobs to gain an advantage).
  • Read your job description carefully for additional rules.
  • Government corruption is only allowed if bribed by Mocro Mafia members.

7. Government & Police Rules

  • The Police Chief leads the PD and answers to the Mayor.
  • Failing to follow superior orders is FailRP and may result in demotion.
  • PD may only KOS if a suspect is armed and posing an immediate threat.
  • Random weapon checks are not allowed.
  • PD may not own personal doors. Their home is the PD station.
  • The first PD door must remain open unless a Mayor-issued lockdown is active.
  • Government officials may not place hits.
  • No baiting players to gain raid justification.
  • PD may block streets only when preparing for a raid and must use proper signage.

8. Communication

  • English and Dutch only.
  • No mic or chat spam (unless your job allows it).
  • No advertising other servers or services.
  • No chain advertising.

9. Job-Specific Rules

  • Hooligan rival groups may RDM each other freely.
  • Street Artists may build a public stage.
  • Terrorists are KOS and may not rejoin the job for 1 hour after death. Must /advert terror before attacking.
  • Hobos may be arrested for public urination after a warning.
  • If being urinated on, you must warn before shooting.
  • Gun Dealers may self-supply only if no other dealer is online, and only one weapon (no shipments).

10. Final Notes

  • Do not change custom job names to misrepresent the role.
  • Bunnyhopping for speed advantage is not allowed.
  • These rules apply to everyone, including staff.
  • Rules may change — staying updated is your responsibility.
