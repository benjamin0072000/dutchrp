Weed GrowOp — Player Guide

The Weed GrowOp system is an immersive and interactive weed production feature that allows players to grow, process, and sell weed using a realistic grow operation. It focuses on long-term planning, equipment management, and risk versus reward gameplay, making it a popular choice for criminal roleplay.

Players can choose between three Weed Grower job variants, including a VIP-exclusive option. Each job has its own mechanics and progression speed. For the best experience, it is strongly recommended to watch the tutorial video for your selected job.

Weed Grower Jobs

Amateur Grower: Entry-level grower job designed for new players. This variant introduces the basic grow mechanics at a slower pace. A tutorial video is available and highly recommended before starting.

Basic Grower: A more advanced grower job with improved production options and faster growth compared to the Amateur Grower. Watching the tutorial video is advised to fully understand the additional mechanics.

Pro Grower (VIP): An exclusive VIP job that offers the most efficient production, advanced upgrades, and higher profit potential. Due to its complexity, watching the tutorial video is strongly recommended.

Growing Weed

Weed production starts by placing grow equipment in a secure location. Plants require proper care and time to grow. Managing your grow operation correctly will directly affect the quality and value of your harvest.

Different job variants unlock different features and efficiencies, making it important to understand your job’s mechanics before scaling up production.

Processing & Packaging

Once the plants are fully grown, they can be harvested and processed. Processing turns raw plants into sellable weed products.

Packaging prepares the weed for transport and sale. Proper processing and packaging increase the final value of the product.

Selling Weed

After packaging, weed can be sold to the appropriate dealer or NPC. Higher quality weed results in better payouts.

Long-running grow operations and large harvests increase risk, as illegal activity may attract law enforcement attention.
