Gang Party

Everything you need to know

What is a gang party?

The Gang Party system allows players to create a temporary party or gang to play together more efficiently. It lets you team up with friends, invite other players, and stay grouped during gameplay. This makes cooperation easier, improves communication, and is perfect for criminal activities or coordinated roleplay situations.

How to use

1. Type !party in chat to open the party menu
2. Create a new party or gang
3. Invite other players to join
4. Play together as a group and manage members through the menu
