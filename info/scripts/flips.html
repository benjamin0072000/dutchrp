Flips & Rolls

Everything you need to know

What are flips and roles?

Flips & Rolls is an in-game gambling feature that allows players to challenge their luck through coin flips and dice rolls. Players can start their own games or join existing ones through a simple menu, making it easy to compete against others in real time. All games are played directly on your screen, creating fast, interactive, and exciting moments without breaking gameplay flow.

How to use

1. Type !flip or !roll in chat to open the menu
2. Create a new flip or roll and choose your bet
3. Join existing games created by other players
4. Watch the outcome play out on your screen
5. Win the pot if luck is on your side — or try again
